Les Carlin

Clinical Psychologist

C.Psychol, A.F.B.Ps.S

BA (Cape Town), BA Hons (Unisa),
MSc (Cape Town), Dip. Family Therapy (IFT London)

Originally from Cape Town, I have been practicing in the the UK since 1981. During that time I have worked in the local NHS mental health services with Children, Adolescents, Adults, and also in the learning disability area. I left NHS service in 2013

I commenced a private practice in 1989. During this time my work with Adults, Children and Adolescents has covered a broad range of problem areas. I have focused on some specific areas such as work stress, eating disorders, obsessive compulsive disorder, post-traumatic stress, and family and couple relationships. Between 1991 and 2007 I had extensive experience working in GP surgeries, accepting a varied range of referrals in that environment. This also included offering consultations to GP surgery staff dealing with their patients' psychological difficulties. I continue to accept referrals locally from GPs.
